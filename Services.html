Welcome to TipToe Soles Services

At TipToe Soles, we believe that proper footwear care goes beyond just buying quality shoes. From professional fitting to custom orthotics, we've got everything covered to enhance your walking experience.

Our Services

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a foot measurement session take?

A professional foot measurement typically takes 20-30 minutes, including the 3D scanning and consultation with our specialist.

How often should I replace my custom orthotics?

Custom orthotics generally last between 1-3 years depending on use, but we recommend annual check-ups to ensure they're still providing optimal support.

Can you stretch all types of shoes?

Most leather and some synthetic materials can be stretched to some degree. During your consultation, our specialists will evaluate if your shoes are suitable for our stretching service.

How long does the shoe repair service take?

Most repair services are completed within 3-7 business days, depending on the type of repair and current workload. For urgent cases, we offer expedited services at an additional cost.

Do I need a referral for gait analysis?

No referral is necessary. Our gait analysis service is available to anyone looking to improve their walking comfort or address foot-related issues.

Are your services covered by insurance?

Some services like custom orthotics may be covered by health insurance plans. We recommend checking with your provider before your appointment. We provide detailed receipts for insurance claim purposes.
