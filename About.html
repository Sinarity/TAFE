Our Story

From trusted manufacturer to Australia's newest comfort footwear brand.

Who We Are

Established in 2010 in Sydney, Australia, TipToe Soles has built a reputation as a trusted footwear manufacturer providing white-label products to many well-known brands. After years of crafting quality shoes for others, we're excited to launch our own brand of affordable comfort footwear designed especially for adults aged 30 and above.

At TipToe Soles, we believe that comfortable footwear shouldn't come with a premium price tag. Our extensive experience as a manufacturer has allowed us to perfect our production processes, source quality materials at competitive prices, and now deliver our own brand directly to Australian consumers and beyond.

Our vision is to become the go-to affordable footwear brand in Australia and around the world. We're starting with what we know best: exceptionally comfortable daily wear shoes that support your everyday life without compromising on quality or style.

Our Values

These core principles guide everything we do at TipToe Soles:

Comfort First

We believe comfortable feet lead to a comfortable life. Every design decision prioritizes how our shoes feel for all-day wear, especially for our 30+ audience.

Affordability

Our direct-to-consumer approach eliminates middlemen, allowing us to offer premium comfort footwear at accessible price points for everyday Australians.

Australian Design

Proudly designed in Sydney, our footwear reflects our understanding of Australian lifestyles, climate, and fashion preferences.

Manufacturing Excellence

With over a decade of manufacturing experience for leading brands, we bring proven production expertise to our own branded footwear.

Inclusive Design

We create shoes for real people with real needs, ensuring our products accommodate various foot shapes and comfort requirements.

Customer Satisfaction

Our easy online shopping experience and responsive customer service reflect our commitment to your complete satisfaction.

Our Journey

A timeline of key milestones in our growth and evolution:

2010

TipToe Soles founded in Sydney as a white-label footwear manufacturer, quickly earning contracts with established shoe brands.

2013

Expanded manufacturing capacity and developed our signature comfort technology featuring advanced memory foam insoles.

2016

Established partnerships with international brands and opened a dedicated research and development facility in Sydney.

2019

Manufactured our one millionth pair of shoes and invested in modernizing our production technology for greater efficiency.

2023

Began development of our own branded line of comfort footwear aimed at Australian consumers aged 30 and above.

2025

Present day: Official launch of TipToe Soles as a consumer brand with our e-commerce website and first collection.

Our Manufacturing Excellence

With over 15 years of experience manufacturing for leading brands, we've perfected the art of creating quality footwear at scale:

Each pair of TipToe Soles comfort shoes is produced in our Sydney facility, where we maintain complete control over quality and consistency. Our manufacturing expertise—previously only available to major brands—is now directly benefiting our customers through affordable, high-quality footwear designed specifically for the 30+ age group who value both comfort and style.

Our production process combines advanced manufacturing technology with key handcrafted elements where the human touch makes a difference. We collaborate with Australian podiatrists to ensure our comfort designs provide proper support for all-day wear. Every pair undergoes rigorous quality testing before receiving our stamp of approval, allowing us to confidently stand behind every product we sell.

Our Online Experience

Launching our own e-commerce website marks an exciting new chapter for TipToe Soles:

After years of manufacturing for others, we're thrilled to connect directly with our customers through our new online store. Our website is designed with simplicity and user experience in mind, making it easy for you to find and purchase the perfect comfort shoes for your needs.

The TipToe Soles online store is fully responsive, ensuring a seamless shopping experience whether you're browsing on your desktop, tablet, or smartphone. We've prioritized website accessibility to ensure all customers can enjoy our products, with our development team adhering to WCAG 2.0 standards.

Our secure payment system, straightforward navigation, and detailed product information are all designed to make your shopping experience as comfortable as our shoes. We'll continue to expand our online offerings as we grow, with plans to introduce new collections and features based on your feedback.
